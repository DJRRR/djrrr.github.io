Hello! I am an Assistant Professor at the [School of Computer Science](http://www.cs.fudan.edu.cn) in Fudan University, where I earned my Ph.D. degree under the guidance of [Prof. Min Yang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=UnKf9FIAAAAJ) and [Prof. Yuan Zhang](https://yuanxzhang.github.io/). My research interests mainly include Vulnerability Detection and Analysis on Critical Targets (e.g., open-source software, Android platform, autonomous driving system, etc.).
